About Us

Founder-Led Excellence Since Day One

Our Founder's Promise

"We don't just send a crew; we bring our family's reputation to your door. Every clean is a reflection of our personal commitment to your peace of mind."

Our Mission

Our mission is to set the gold standard in professional cleaning by combining industrial-grade precision with the care and dedication of a family-run business.

We are committed to using eco-friendly, non-toxic products that ensure a healthy environment for your family and employees, delivered through our rigorous 50-point quality checklist that leaves no corner untouched.

Beyond just basic cleaning, we aim to revitalize every space we enter. By utilizing the latest industry extraction technologies and sanitization protocols, we provide a level of deep cleaning that goes beneath the surface, ensuring a literally spotless and medically-safe atmosphere for your peace of mind.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and recommended name in property maintenance across the Lower Mainland, known for our unwavering quality and personal touch.

Our vision is built on the foundation of personal founder-led oversight and the goal of setting new benchmarks for cleanliness and professional service in every community we serve, from Surrey to Vancouver.

We envision a future where Tiwana Bro'z is synonymous with "Elite Cleaning." By constantly innovating our methods and expanding our specialized service offerings, we aim to lead the industry in sustainability and client satisfaction, becoming the first choice for both residential estates and large-scale commercial facilities.

Our Story

Started by Harinder and Satinder Tiwana with a passion for cleanliness, we are a fully licensed operation dedicated to serving homeowners and businesses with excellence.

As active founders who personally audit and oversee every project, we bring an unmatched level of energy and professional integrity to the industry, ensuring every space we touch reflects our personal standard of perfection and family values.

What began as a localized effort to provide better service has grown into a premium brand known for reliability. We have invested in the best equipment and the most thorough training to ensure that our family-first philosophy is felt in every room we clean, resulting in lasting relationships with our clients.

Founder-Led Quality

We believe that elite cleaning requires elite oversight. Unlike larger franchises where owners are distant, Harinder and Satinder are on the front lines personally.

This hands-on philosophy is why we can guarantee 100% satisfaction. We don't just send a crew; we bring our professional integrity to your door. Every clean is a reflection of our dedication to your peace of mind and the health of your environment.

Our "Platinum Standard" isn't just a slogan—it's a rigorous auditing process performed by the founders themselves. From the initial walkthrough to the final inspection, we ensure that every square inch of your property meets the high-energy, detail-oriented standards we set for ourselves.
