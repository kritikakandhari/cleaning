The Ultimate Move-Out Checklist

April 10, 2026 8 min read

Moving property is notoriously chaotic. When you're managing logistics and packing boxes, the deep extraction cleaning of your previous home is often the last priority—yet it's the most critical for securing your deposit.

Our professional checklist ensures that no corner is overlooked, guaranteeing a seamless transition and landlord approval every single time.

The Kitchen Protocol

  • Defrost the freezer and thoroughly wipe down the inside of the refrigerator.
  • Scrub the oven interior completely—don't forget the underlying warming drawer!
  • Disinfect the sink, garbage disposal, and completely wipe down all cabinet shelves (inside and out).

Sanitary Standards

  • Bleach and scrub the shower grout to eradicate all mildew and surface staining.
  • Wash all glass mirrors and shower doors with a streak-free professional formula.
  • Sanitize the toilet bowl, tank, and surrounding base meticulously.
"The difference between getting a partial deposit and a full deposit lies in the details that you only see once the furniture is gone."

General Living Areas

  • Dust all ceiling fans, lighting fixtures, and high-reach corners for cobwebs.
  • Wipe down all window sills and wash the interior glass panes until perfectly clear.
  • Vacuum and aggressively mop all floors, ensuring to clean underneath where furniture used to sit.
