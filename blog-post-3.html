The Ultimate Move-Out Checklist

Ensure you get your deposit back every single time.

Helping you transition smoothly.

Moving out is notoriously chaotic. Between packing boxes and organizing logistics, deep cleaning the property you are leaving is usually the last thing on your mind. However, landlords and property managers inspect thoroughly. Follow our professional checklist to ensure you leave the property spotless.

The Kitchen

  • Defrost the freezer and thoroughly wipe down the inside of the refrigerator.
  • Scrub the oven interior completely—don't forget the underlying warming drawer!
  • Disinfect the sink, garbage disposal, and completely wipe down all cabinet shelves (inside and out).

The Bathrooms

  • Bleach and scrub the shower grout to eradicate all mildew.
  • Wash the mirrors with a streak-free formula.
  • Sanitize the toilet bowl, tank, and surrounding base thoroughly.

General Living Areas

  • Dust all ceiling fans, lighting fixtures, and high corners for cobwebs.
  • Wipe down all window sills and wash the interior glass panes.
  • Vacuum and aggressively mop all floors, ensuring to clean underneath where furniture used to sit.

Too overwhelmed by packing?

You focus on moving to your new home, and we’ll handle the mess you leave behind. Tiwana Bro'z specializes in end-of-tenancy cleans that guarantee landlord satisfaction.
